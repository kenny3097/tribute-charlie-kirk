Charlie Kirk

Charlie Kirk was an American activist and founder of Turning Point USA. He became a prominent political commentator and author, inspiring young leaders across the united states and around the world to engage in activism and politics.He was fatally assassinated on 10th September 2025 while speaking to students at Utah Valley University.He will always be remembered for his profound speeches and encouraging dialogue.

Achievements

  • Founded Turning Point USA at just 18 years old.
  • Author of several books including "Campus Battlefield".
  • Frequent guest on national media as a political commentator.
